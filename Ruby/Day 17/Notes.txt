Undercore(_) :
    - It is special type of variable that stores the last obtained answer in it
      Ex: a = 5
          a + 6   # 11
          _ * 3   # 33

Order 
    - ' ', '!', '\', '#', '$', '%', '&', ''', '(', ')', '*', '+', ',', '-', '.', '/', '0'...
    - ':', ';', '<', '=', '>', '?', '@', 'A'..., 'AA'...
    - '^', '_', '`', 'a'...'aa'...
